Our Beginning

Everyday in our day to day lives, we see small children begging on roadside asking for money and destroying their livelihood. Why don’t they go to orphanages so that they improve their living conditions. Also, adoption process is very smooth, transparent and reliable in foreign countries but not so much same in India. So, to enhance these two main problems, we came up with the idea of Kidolio

Our Mission

Kidolio is a website which aims to help the orphanages and help the orphans find their families. Kidolio is just about every child who dreams of calling someone mom and dad but that dream doesn’t becomes reality. Kidolio is their to make lives better for orphans and find their homes. Our mission also includes helping the children who beg on streets find orphanages so that they can move towards a better life

Our Vision

We aim to build more orphanages so that every child gets a proper livelihood and doesn’t have to beg on streets. We aim to improve the living conditions of the orphans in orphanages so that they get a better lifestyle. Our vision also includes to make people more aware about adoption so that more children are able to find their sweet homes
